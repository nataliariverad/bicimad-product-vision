The current context

Madrid back.

Painful onboarding

  • 01Requires a Spanish phone number to register
  • 02Credit card must be added via external browser
  • 03App unavailable on Google Play outside Spain
  • 04English translation is subpar and confusing
  • 05Multiple unnecessary steps deter new users

Fragmented transport

  • 01Metro card — separate registration and top-up
  • 02BiciMAD — completely separate app and billing
  • 03EMT buses — own system, own subscription
  • 04Airport — top-up only at Aeropuerto T4 station
  • 05No single card works across all modes

Phase 01 · Onboarding

Fix the front door.

Remove every barrier between a new user and their first ride. Still BiciMAD — but a BiciMAD that works for Felipe on his first day in the city.

Fix 01

Any phone number

Register with any international number — no Spanish SIM required.

Fix 02

Valid passport

Passport accepted as ID — removes the NIE barrier for newcomers.

Fix 03

In-app payment

Add card directly in the app — no external browser redirect.

Fix 04

Apple & Google Pay

One-tap wallet payment — faster, safer, already in every pocket.

Fix 05

Global app access

Remove the locale restriction on Google Play — any country can download.

Fix 06

English first

Full English UX overhaul — tourists and expats feel at home.

Phase 03 · Platform & Ecosystem

Build the Ecosystem.

Bikes become the first touchpoint. Hotels, museums, restaurants and tech partners benefit alongside — and become reasons to come back.

Hotels

Pre-load MadUnlocked at check-in. Pass is on the phone before the bag is unpacked.

Museums & galleries

Discounted entry and priority access for MadUnlocked pass holders.

Biking routes

Curated neighborhood tours — La Latina, Malasaña, Retiro — built into the app.

Google Maps

Bike routes surface as a transit option inside Google Maps — search a destination, tap to ride.

Restaurants & shops

Pass-holder discounts and perks across Madrid's food and retail scene.

Airports

Pre-arrival pass activation at Barajas — passport scan, ready to ride at landing.

Madrid Tourism Board

Co-branded launch. Longer stays, more spend, global brand lift for the city.

Events & nightlife

Concert, festival and venue partner perks — Madrid after dark, one tap away.

This is not a transport app. It is Madrid city membership.
