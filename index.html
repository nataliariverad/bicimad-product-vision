Phase 01 · Onboarding

Fix the front door.

Remove every barrier between a new user and their first ride. Still BiciMAD — but a BiciMAD that works for Felipe on his first day in the city.

TimelineQ3 2026
Fix 01

Any phone number

Register with any phone number — no Spanish number needed.

Fix 02

Improve UX

Remove unnecessary steps and keep the whole experience in the app — no external browsing.

Fix 03

In-app payments

Apple Pay & Google Pay integrated as buttons inside the app — top up without leaving it.

Fix 04

Improve translation

Polished English so native English speakers can navigate with ease.

Phase 02 · One Pass

One Madrid, one pass.

A single digital NFC pass for bikes, buses and metro. In Apple Wallet and Google Wallet. No new card to carry, nothing new to download.

Phase 03 · Platform & Ecosystem

Build the ecosystem.

Bikes become the first touchpoint for people exploring and falling in love with Madrid. Local businesses benefit alongside, becoming reasons to come back.

TimelineQ1–Q2 2027
01

Introduce a Tourist Card

A one-day free pass for tourists. Partner with hotels to distribute it at check-in.

02

Google Maps integration

When users search a route, the bike option includes a button straight to the MadOne app.

03

Introduce biking routes

Curated neighborhood tours to discover the city — La Latina, Malasaña, Retiro.
